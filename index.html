I'm Baishakhi Pal

I am presently pursuing Ph.D in Department of Physics from Jadavpur University, Kolkata and a DST-INSPIRE fellow there. I am working in the field of ‘Surface Modification, Fabrication and Characterization of Crystalline Silicon Solar Cells’. I have been extensively involved in optimiza- tion of different parameters for varying sheet resistance of emitter surface in c-Si solar cells, by both experimental as well as simulation. Broader area of my research includes Crystalline Silicon Selective Emitter Solar Cells using Laser Doping, double-Diffusion and Etch-back process. I am keen to work sincerely with dedication and perseverance in scientific research and development especially in the field of Photovoltaics. I have previously worked as a MNRE, GOI project fellow on the project entitled "Development of High Efficiency, Selective Emitter, Al2O3 Passivated, Large Area n-type Crystalline Silicon Solar Cell" in Meghnad Saha Institute of Technology, Kolkata.
